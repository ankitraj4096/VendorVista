Our Story

Sharma Chaat started as a humble family stall more than 25 years ago, dedicated to serving authentic and hygienic chaat dishes. Our recipes are handed down through generations, and we pride ourselves on fresh ingredients, clean preparation spaces, and friendly service. Over the years, we have grown but never compromised on the quality and trust our customers expect. Come taste the tradition!
